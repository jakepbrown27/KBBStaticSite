About Us

We bring a polished, professional bar experience to weddings, private parties, and corporate events — without the stress. From signature cocktails to smooth service and great guest energy, our goal is simple: make your event feel effortless and elevated.

We are a dry mobile bartending service; we legally cannot supply the alcohol, meaning we provide the bartending, bar setup, mixers, garnishes, tools, and everything needed to run a beautiful bar — but we do not supply the alcohol. We will absolutely provide a detailed, curated list of items to purchase for your event. This keeps things simple and compliant with local laws while giving you full control over what is served.

Whether you’re hosting an intimate backyard celebration or a large-scale event, we’ll help you plan the menu, guide you on what to purchase, and deliver a clean, friendly, and professional bar experience from start to finish.
